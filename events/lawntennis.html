Lawn Tennis

* The matches of the initial rounds will comprise of best of 15 games with two singles and one doubles in the order first singles, doubles, second singles.
* At 7 each, the match will be decided by the tie break.
* The matches will be of best of three sets from the semi finals.
* The results of the full match shall be declared when either of them wins two matches.
* The tennis Tie Break Rules shall be applied in all the sets (7/12 points).
* The number of players representing an institute shall not be less than two or more than four.
* Not more than four certificates shall be awarded to a team.
