Volleyball

* Every team shall submit a list of players not exceeding 12, who are expected to participate in the tournament.
* All the matches shall be played on the best of five sets.
* Teams must reach the venue at the specified time.
* If a team is late, opponent team will be given walkover.
* Any team refusing to play match without justification, will be eliminated from the competition.
* The Referees will have the final say in case of any dispute.
* Internationally accepted rules will be followed in the tournament.
