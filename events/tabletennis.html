Table Tennis

  • The rules of the International Table Tennis Federation as adopted from time to time by the Table Tennis Federation of India shall apply unless otherwise modified in these rules.
    MEN: Matches will be held on the basis of five singles (Best of five)
    WOMEN: Matches will be held on the basis of two singles and one doubles (Best of three)
  • The order of play shall be as follows:Three players on one side are numbers A,B and C andthe three players on the other side are numbered X,Y and Z.
    A X
    B Y
    C Z
      • Order of play shall be:
      1.		A		vs.			X
      2.		B		vs.			Y
      3.		C		vs.			Z
      4.		A		vs.			Y
      5.		B		vs.			X

    • The results of the tie shall be declared when either of the team wins three matches.
    • The number of players representing any institute shall not be less than three or more than four.
      At most four certificates shall be awarded to a winning team.
    • Each singles shall be played on best of five games basis, each game consist of 11 points.
    • The number of players representing an institute shall not be less than two or more than three in the women?s event.
    • Not more than 3 certificates shall be awarded to a team.
