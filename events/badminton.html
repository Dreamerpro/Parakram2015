Badminton

* The competition shall be conducted under the International Badminton Federation rules as adopted from time to time by the badminton Association of India, unless modified in these rules.
* The tournament for men and women section will be conducted in the lines of Inter State Badminton Championship.
* According to this pattern, each tie separately of men and women should consist of:
* The tie for men shall be decided by the combined result of 3 singles and 2 doubles and for Women by the results of 2 singles and one doubles.
* The result of a tie however can be declared when either of the competing teams wins 3 matches in men`s section and 2 matches in the women`s section.
* Result (Men and Women) of best of three sets (each set of 21 points)

The order of events shall be as follows:
* Men: First Singles/Second Singles/First Doubles/Third Singles/Second Double
* Women: First Singles/Second Singles/Doubles

Number of players
* Men: The number of players representing any institute in a team will be a maximum of 5 members.
* Women: The number of players representing any institute in a team will be a maximum of 3 members.
