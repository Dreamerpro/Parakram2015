AMATEUR BOXING

  • Rules:
  •  Boxing is a combat sport in which points are awarded according to the clean punches hit by the players. All players must be senior student of any registered university. There will be 2 to 3 rounds each of 2-3 minutes. All other rules are same as followed by Boxing Federation of India. Decisions of the referee and judges will be final. A total of 10 weight categories, 8 categories for boys and two for girls will be there, namely:

    Medals:

    For each category, gold for winner, silver for runner up, one bronze medal for 3rd position. There will be more than 4 players in each category. Total of 30 medals in 10 categories(8 for boys and 2 for girls). 

    Trophy: 

    One trophy for the “Best Player in Boxing” must be given at the award function ceremony.


    Venue:

    SAC, Boxing room or open area.
