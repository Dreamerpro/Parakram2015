Athletics

* Every individual must hold a valid college identification card.
* In case of team entries, all team members must be from the same college.
* Every individual brings its own its own personal gear.
* Common equipments to be used in the competition will be provided by ISM Dhanbad.
* Maximum 3 entries are allowed for each event from one college and one participant can do maximum of 3 events apart from relays.
* Whatever is to be used in the competition has to be verified by the referees.
* Depending upon the number of participants, events can have heats, semi-finals and/or direct finals.
* Individual events may be cancelled and events might be rescheduled subject to less than 10 entries per event.
* All international rules to apply.
* All participants must be registered at least a day before in order to compete and report half an hour before the scheduled time failing which their participation is liable to be cancelled.
* Any misconduct will be strictly dealt with.
* All officials and referees will be the final authority in case of any disputes.
